What is Venued?

Have you ever driven to another city to see a band and found out that the cheapest parking near the venue ends up costing you $25? Or have you ever Ubered to a stadium with a handbag, only to find out that the venue only allows clear bags?

Venued is a venue-focused website, aimed at helping users submit tips about their favorite places to help out other venue-explorers (or 'Venueders' as we like to call them!)

Users can tell other people where the best place to park is, how early to get to the venue if an event is super popular, best restaurants to eat at after the concert is over, and much more!
